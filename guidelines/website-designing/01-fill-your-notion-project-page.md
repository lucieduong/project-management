# Step 1: Fill your Notion project page

Do this **first**. Open your project in the Website Dev Pipeline and fill in the sections below on that page. The AI will use this information in Step 2 to build your Figma Make prompt.

---

## Where to do it

1. Open the **Website Dev Pipeline** board in Notion.  
2. Open the **project page** for the website you’re designing (click the project card).  
3. Fill in the sections listed below. Save when you’re done.

---

## What to fill in (use the sections on your project page)

### Client & project
- **Domain or site name** — e.g. “Acme Catering”  
- **Type of business** — e.g. “Local food service” or “Ecommerce”  
- **Goal of the site in one line** — e.g. “Get more quote requests from schools and events”

### Research (Planning)
- **Top 3 keywords (from SEMrush)** — e.g. “school catering”, “event catering near me”, “corporate catering”  
- **Competitor 1** — Full website URL + in a few words what to take from them (e.g. “Hero layout and services grid; trust badges above footer”)  
- **Competitor 2** — Full website URL + what to take (e.g. “Clear ‘Get a quote’ button and contact form placement”)

### Design inputs
- **Client website (if they already have one)** — URL  
- **Brand colors** — Hex codes or color names (e.g. “Green #2D5016, white, dark gray”)  
- **Logo** — Upload or add a link (e.g. “In project folder”)  
- **Fonts (if you know them)** — e.g. “Inter, Georgia”  
- **Screenshots or references** — Any inspiration images or links

### Pages to design
- List the pages or sections you need — e.g. “Home, About, Menu, Contact” or “Home (hero, services, menu preview, contact)”

### Style (if not already on the page)
- **2–3 words** for the look and feel — e.g. “Modern and clean”, “Warm and local”, “Premium minimal”

---

## When you’re done

- Save the page.  
- **Copy the page URL** (from your browser’s address bar). You’ll paste this into the prompt in **Step 2**.  
- Go to [02-get-figma-make-prompt-in-cursor.md](02-get-figma-make-prompt-in-cursor.md).
